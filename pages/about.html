About

Quick Introduction

Hi! I'm Ethan and I'm currently a second year student at the University of Sussex studying Computer Science and Artificial intelligence.


I'm interested in quite a lot of things computer science, but my main interests revolve around Game Development, and Artificial Intelligence.


Why I picked Computer Science

I remember when I was a kid, my dad introduced me to the concepts of programming with Arduino. I was pretty excited about programming but could just not wrap my head around what it meant when the compiler kept saying that my variable needs to be declared, so I stopped trying to learn it.


In high school, I was quite interested in physical Engineering. However, I felt like there was less opportunity to express my creativity in that field so I got more and more interested in computer science. Now I sometimes find myself coding for weeks and time would just whizz by.


Hobbies

I would say that I'm a pretty creative person. I really like creating things whether it's for me or for other people.


I spend a lot of time doing things like songwriting, singing and music production in my free time, as well as software development.

Where do I see myself in 10 years

I don't really know, but I definitely see myself making more cool stuff!
